Artemis Mission Timeline

The Artemis Mission Timeline details key milestones from Artemis I through Artemis IV, outlining NASA’s path to returning humans to the Moon. It includes critical phases such as:

  • Artemis I: Uncrewed test flight to lunar orbit (completed successfully)
  • Artemis II: First crewed mission around the Moon
  • Artemis III: Lunar landing with the first woman and person of color
  • Artemis IV: Gateway module assembly for long-term lunar presence

Artemis Mission Projects

The Artemis program involves multiple international and industrial collaborations that integrate technologies for sustainable space exploration. Major projects include:

  • Orion Spacecraft: Deep-space crew module for lunar missions.
  • SLS Rocket: Most powerful rocket built for human spaceflight.
  • Lunar Gateway: Modular space station orbiting the Moon.
  • Human Landing System: Designed for safe and reusable Moon landings.
